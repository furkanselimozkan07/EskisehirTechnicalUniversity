Welcome to Eskisehir Technical University

Eskisehir Technical University, established by Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018, is located in the center of the vibrant and youthful city of Eskisehir. It operates on 3 campuses and includes 5 faculties, 3 vocational schools, 1 higher education school, 3 institutes, and 8 research and application centers.

Eskisehir Technical University is committed to providing a creative and dynamic educational environment to prepare its students to become highly skilled and competitive individuals. Our faculty members, who are experts in their fields, devote all their time to sharing their knowledge with students.

Our University operates the international Hasan Polatkan Airport located at the 2nd September Campus, managed by the Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences. There are international flights to and from this airport by various airlines on different days of the week.
