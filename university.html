Welcome to Eskisehir Technical University

About Us

Eskisehir Technical University, established by Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018, is located in the heart of the dynamic and youthful city of Eskisehir. The university operates across three campuses and includes 6 faculties, 3 vocational schools, 1 higher education school, 3 institutes, and 8 research and application centers.

The university is committed to providing a creative and dynamic educational environment to its students, preparing them to be highly skilled, competitive individuals in their respective fields. Our faculty members, who are experts in their fields, devote all their time to sharing knowledge with students in a student-centered environment.

Eskisehir Technical University also manages the international Hasan Polatkan Airport at the 2nd September Campus, operated by the Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences. International flights are conducted by different airlines on various days of the week.

Numbers of Students and Staff

Our university currently serves over 14,392 students and employs more than 1,598 academic and administrative staff members.
