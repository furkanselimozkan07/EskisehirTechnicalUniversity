Campus Facilities

Eskisehir Technical University provides its students with a vibrant and dynamic campus life. The campus offers a variety of services to support both academic and social activities. Some of the key features of our campus include:

  • Convenient Market: A market located inside the campus, where students can find essential items for their daily needs.
  • ATMs: All major banks have their ATMs available across the campus for students' convenience.
  • Cafes and Canteens: There are several cafes and canteens where students can relax and enjoy meals or snacks between classes.
  • Libraries: The campus is equipped with two well-stocked libraries, providing students with access to a wide range of academic resources and study spaces.
